Film Why Shoot It?

Film photography stands out for its unique texture and depth, compelling photographers to engage deeply with each shot. Its natural grain imbues images with a soulful quality that digital formats often lack. The developing process adds anticipation, turning every photo into a carefully crafted piece of art. This combination of tactile artistry and unpredictability makes film a cherished medium for authentic expressions.

About the Artist Mitchel Franks

Born and raised in the heart of the Utah Rocky Mountains, I developed a profound love for nature and film photography, capturing the untamed beauty of the wild through the lens of a camera. Now, the goal is to become a travel film photographer, immortalizing the world's diverse landscapes and cultures with the same passion and authenticity.
